Kitchen Cabinets Remodeling

Kitchen remodeling was a large stress for me

I actually stumbled on a kitchen cabinet design program by accident. I would never have guessed that there was a program on line to model kitchen cabinet designs.

I mean discuss a niche market! Nonetheless, there it was. It only cost me a few bucks a month, and it was a pretty useful tool. It was a little bit hard to learn at first, but once I got the hang of it, it wasn't bad.

Basically,It was a simplified architectural design program - made for individuals like me who don't know much about computers. They must have had some deals with a kitchen cabinet designer somewhere.

The program could model almost all the popular kitchen cabinet designs on www.customcabinetsnc.com It even had mock ups of refrigerators, kitchen islands, and basic shapes of kitchen tables.

It wasn't complete, but it was definitely good enough to give me an idea of what my kitchen would look like with various design elements. I finally picked out a perfect kitchen cabinet design.

I decide to go with a dark oak. I picked out some pretty low profile kitchen cabinets. At first, I had been tempted to get big types with a lot of storage room, but then I thought better of it.

I wanted everything to have a very sleek, angular, minimal look to it. My kitchen was big enough that I would have enough storage space regardless, and I'm tall enough to get to the upper cabinets without much of a problem.

That allowed me to use smaller kitchen cabinet designs, which in turn made the kitchen feel even less cluttered. All in all, it was a great effect. As a matter of fact, when I was done it looked precisely like the program!
